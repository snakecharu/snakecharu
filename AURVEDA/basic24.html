VATA-PITTA

A Vata-Pitta constitution means the individual has a dominant combination of Vata (Air + Space) and Pitta (Fire + Water) doshas. This type blends the quickness, creativity, and flexibility of Vata with the intensity, focus, and determination of Pitta.

A Vata-Kapha constitution combines the lightness and mobility of Vata (Air + Space) with the heaviness and stability of Kapha (Earth + Water). This dual type is a blend of creativity and calmness, but may have opposing traits that require balance.
